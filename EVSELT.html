EVSE Load Tester

The number of electric vehicles sold and EVSEs installed is increasing annually, and there could be multiple vehicles charging on one station. The charging stations, however, may have current overloading due to the energy demand from the multiple vehicles, which will make the EVSE difficult to supply energy to the vehicles.

Technicians will need to figure out how to observe these changes to see if an EVSE would need repairing. Therefore, our team will implement an EVSE test device that will measure the EVSE supply, test the ground fault circuit interrupter, and test load balancing by building a load bank.

The measurements will be stored on an external device or an online database. The tester will be built to all standards specified by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) J1772 publications.
